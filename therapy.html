Let's Talk Therapy

Background: My client is a counsellor based in Glen Innes. We worked together to make sure that the website and branding for the business to look as professional as possible while maintaining some personal touch specified by my client.

Expertise
Web Design & Development, Logo & Branding

Year
2022



Techonogies:



Visit let's talk therapy

Goal:

With the use of online appointments, it is now simpler than ever to connect with your mental health provider. Today's mental health services are more competitive than ever. Outstanding client experiences, both online and offline, are necessary to remain current and ahead of the competition. ​

I think a website should accurately reflect the character and principles of its client. It will help the potential client know who they are dealing with even before they start their road towards mental health.


Process:

In order to help potential clients better understand their counselor's personality, values, and essential competencies, we decided together what pages and features the website should have. After deciding on the fundamental components of the web pages, we choose the colours. The word "growth" serves as the inspiration for the logo, which is coloured blue to represent security and tranquilly. As our minds continue to develop, grow, and learn, so does the logo. To appeal to a younger audience, flat colours and rounded shapes have been used.


Results:

I worked very closely with my client to make sure I covered all of the technical cases they could think of, and make sure that everything could technically work throughout the checkout experience. Once I “handed off” the designs, our communication continued and my client is very happy with the results!​
