## 🌟 Key Features

- 🎯 **Diamond Hero Mini-Game** – New gameplay mode with leaderboard-driven competition and over 1.4 million plays.
- 🎨 **Remastered Graphics** – Updated character designs by Muchang increase appeal and user engagement.
- 🤝 **Community & Multiplayer** – Taiwan’s team challenges, group emojis, and leaderboards boost collaboration and friendly rivalry.
- 📱 **Circle to Search** – Japan’s unique interactive quest system, leveraging Android’s Circle to Search feature for immersive gameplay.
- 🎁 **Sweepstakes System** – Flexible auto/manual entry options with gamified UI, resulting in 3.7 million entries and 172,000+ registrations.

---

## 🛠 My Role

I was one of the front-end developers responsible for building the website components of Diamond Valley 2024, including the homepage and sweepstakes pages. For the Japan version, I developed the gacha popup feature, enhancing player interaction and excitement.

---

## ✨ Evolution & Impact

Originally crafted for Korea in 2023, the campaign was expanded for Taiwan and Japan with custom features tailored to each region’s audience. The blend of role-play game mechanics, interactive quests, and community-driven rewards powered a 2.7x increase in registrations and heightened player engagement across markets.
