> Whether it’s celebrating wins, encouraging peers, or just sending a virtual coffee ☕, this app helps teams stay connected and appreciated — even when working remotely.

## 🌟 Key Features

- 💌 **Send Personalized Kudos** – Celebrate teammates with gratitude messages, coffee, or gift cards.
- 🏆 **Track Top MVPs** – Discover who gives and receives the most appreciation.
- 📊 **See Culture Insights** – Get a visual pulse on your team’s appreciation habits.
- 🤖 **AI Suggestions** – Let AI help you write heartfelt notes with just one click!

---

## ✨ Why This Matters

In fast-moving workspaces, it's easy to forget to acknowledge one another.
AppreciateYa makes it easy to express gratitude and positivity — improving morale, motivation, and human connection across teams.

---

## 🧠 New Feature: AI Kudos Writer

Too tired to write but want to show you care?
Let the built-in **AI Kudos Generator** write a sweet message on your behalf — thoughtful and clever in seconds.
