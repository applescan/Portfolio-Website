> Created for the 2024 Tokyo Game Show, **Diamond Castle** is a vertical speed runner and mobile-friendly mini-game developed in collaboration with Google Play Japan. It blends retro flair, modern branding, and event-based interaction into a vibrant online experience.

- 💻 [View Reel on Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w1Huo7F7S4I&list=TLGGfN2m5Wtxa9swOTA3MjAyNQ)

---

## 🌟 Key Highlights

- 🏰 **Original Mini-Game** – A vertical climber game with smooth mobile controls and high replay value.
- 📱 **Online + On-Site** – Built to extend the Tokyo Game Show experience beyond the exhibit hall.
- 🎁 **Reward System** – Leaderboard ranks, social sharing, and early sign-ups unlocked exclusive giveaways.
- 🤝 **Cross-Studio Collaboration** – Designed in partnership with Kayac and Hakuhodo for Google Play Japan.
- 🎮 **Game Dev Collabs** – Integrated content from top developers like HoYoverse and Bandai Namco.

---

## 🛠 My Role

As a frontend developer on the team, I helped build the website interface and ensure a seamless experience across devices. This included implementing the homepage layout, optimizing the game’s mobile performance, and collaborating closely with designers to bring the vibrant retro look to life.

---

## 🔧 The Process

- 🧭 **User Flow & Wireframing** – Established clear content structure and intuitive player journey from landing to game.
- 🧱 **Asset Refinement** – Ensured pixel-perfect placement of characters, objects, and collision zones.
- 🎨 **Visual Balance** – Merged Google Play’s bold identity with Diamond Castle’s playful, nostalgic style.
- 🧩 **Iterative Collaboration** – Worked side-by-side with Japanese designers and developers to align gameplay feel with real-world event excitement.
