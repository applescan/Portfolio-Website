Mana - Power of Aotearoa

Through the creation of social media and digital content as well as brand ideation and idealisation, this project will put my branding communications talents to the test.

Expertise
Identity & Branding, Print

Year
2019



Techonogies:



Download Brand Concept

This design is inspired by New Zealand nature, with the simplistic design I want to portray a more stripped-back and natural feeling of the product. The focus is on product descriptions, with no disruptive illustrations or logos so consumers can understand what the product is in one glance. All the package is recyclable, with paper pouch packaging as an option as well.
The name Mana is chosen because of its meaning and its close bond to Kiwis. Mana means a source of both personal and collective strength. Keeping it simple and catchy is key for customers to remember the brand.


Process:

  • Brief: Making a New Zealand Inspired whey protein packaging and social media campaign.

  • Design: Simplistic minimalist design that incorporates New Zealand ingenuity.

  • Design iteration and internal feedback


With this as my starting point, to do a great job at each prompt and get the best results, I needed to:


  • Research Kiwi culture and design moods, get internal alignment on brand goals and learn more about how we want the brand to be perceived by the audience.

  • Design a logo that truly captures the spirit of "Mana"

  • Identify what changes and additions could be made to make it easier for a potential customer to better understand what the brand has done, its values, and its core ingredients.


Results:

Mana has established a look and brand guidelines that truly show New Zealand's strength. The "Power of Aotearoa" would ensure the brand has a unique, consistent, recognizable message at every touchpoint.
